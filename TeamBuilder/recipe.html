Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • i pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 deg for one hour.
  4. Eat and enjoy!
